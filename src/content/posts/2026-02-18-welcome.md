This is my digital garden — a place where I keep track of things I'm building, watching, reading, and learning about.

## Why a digital garden?

Unlike a polished blog, a digital garden is meant to be a living, evolving collection of notes. Some posts will be rough drafts, some will be short thoughts, and some might grow into something bigger over time.

## What to expect

- **Created** — projects, code, art, and things I've built
- **Watched** — movies, shows, talks, and videos worth noting
- **Read** — books, articles, and papers that caught my attention
- **Learned** — concepts, techniques, and things I figured out

Let's see what grows.
